### Research Interests

My research focuses on:
- **Factuality in LLMs**: Understanding and improving the factual accuracy of large language models
- **Model Interpretability**: Analyzing attention mechanisms and internal representations
- **Vision-Language Models**: Exploring reasoning capabilities and spatial understanding in VLMs
- **Hallucination Mitigation**: Developing methods to detect and reduce hallucinations in generative AI
